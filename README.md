<h2>🚀 Project Features</h2>

<h3>For Customers</h3>
<ul>
  <li><b>AI Chat Bot:</b> Integrated smart assistant to provide instant plant care advice and customer support.</li>
  <li><b>Browse & Shop:</b> High-quality catalog with detailed plant info.</li>
  <li><b>Secure Auth:</b> Safe registration and login processes.</li>
  <li><b>Order History:</b> Track your plant journey and past purchases.</li>
</ul>

<h3>For Admins</h3>
<ul>
  <li><b>Dashboard:</b> Overview of store performance.</li>
  <li><b>Inventory Control:</b> Manage plant listings with ease.</li>
  <li><b>Order Management:</b> Real-time status tracking (Pending/Shipped/Delivered).</li>
  <li><b>User Oversight:</b> Manage and track registered customers.</li>
</ul>
