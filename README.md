# 🛒 GreenMart – Online Organic Store & Management System

## 📌 Project Overview

**GreenMart** is a web-based e-commerce platform designed to bridge the gap between fresh produce suppliers and consumers. The system provides a centralized digital environment where customers can browse and purchase fresh organic products, and store managers can efficiently manage the product inventory and customer orders.

This platform simplifies the shopping experience by providing a clean, responsive interface and an organized management system.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Provide an easy-to-use online platform for customers to purchase fresh produce.
* Simplify inventory management for store administrators.
* Provide a secure and efficient ordering system.
* Improve the accessibility of organic products for consumers.

---

## 👥 System Users

### 1. Customers
Customers can:
* Register and log in to the system.
* Browse and search for fresh products.
* Add items to the shopping cart.
* Place orders and track order status.
* Manage their personal profile.

### 2. Store Administrators
Administrators can:
* Manage product listings (Add, Update, Delete).
* View and manage customer orders.
* Monitor inventory levels.
* Manage user accounts.

---

## 🧩 System Modules

### Authentication Module
Handles user registration, login, and secure session management.

### Product Management Module
Allows admins to manage the product catalog.
* Add/Update/Delete product details.
* Categorize products (Fruits, Vegetables, etc.).

### Order Management Module
Handles the end-to-end shopping process.
* View cart items.
* Place orders.
* Update order status.

---

## 🗄️ Database Entities

* **User:** Stores customer and admin credentials.
* **Product:** Details of the organic items available.
* **Order:** Customer purchase history and order details.
* **Cart:** Items selected by the user before checkout.
